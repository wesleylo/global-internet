Facebook and Google, along with many other social-minded tech
companies, have put into efforts to increase worldwide internet
access. Ideas include building giant balloons or drones, capable
of flying for extended periods of time, that beam down
Internet access.
While the costs of developing these technologies and providing free
or low cost Internet to so many people are huge, Internet access
will reshape the economies of the world as a whole. We need to
support these efforts of providing global internet access. Entirely new
industries will be created, and, alongside that, opportunity will
be offered to billions with the spread of knowledge and
organization of social interactions.
